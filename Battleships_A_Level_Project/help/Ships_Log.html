Battleships Game Ships Log

Navigating the ships log.

Read the instructions below or watch the video on how to navigate the ships log.

Please note that in order to record statistics you must first have completeted a full game. If you do not finish the game then your stats will not be recorded

Read instructions below or watch the video at the end of this page to learn more about the ships log navigation.

Instructions:

1 Your Profile

This screen shows the main player their statistics from previous games and where they lie in the overall positioning of all games.

2 Least Hits

This screen compares the least number of hits taken to win a game the player with the lowest hits will be the top of the leaderboard.

3 Least Time

This screen compares the least amount of time taken to win a game the player with the shortest time will be the top of the leaderboard. Time is measured only while it is your turn clock freezes as soon as you press next player.

4 Most Wins

This screen displays the player with the greatest number of wins overall. The player with the highest wins will be at the top of the leaderboard.

5 Exit

press this button to leave the ships log screen and go back to the options menu.

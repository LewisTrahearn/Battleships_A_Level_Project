Battleships Game Help

How to play the game.

Read the instructions below or watch the video on how to play the battleships game.

Please note that in order to Play against the CPU, you must first have logged in to the system. If you have not done this then please register first and make sure you are logged in. To play against someone else plesae ensure both players are logged in.

in order to learn to play this game against both the CPU and other players read the following or watch the video below.

Instructions:

1 Rules

The rules of Battleships are quite simple

  • The first player must pick a coordinate from the randomly generated grid
  • next player 1 will fire upon the chosen coordinate.
  • Whatever the outcome of this shot (hit or miss) the player must then hand control over to their opponent
  • The opponent then repeats this process until either player gets a hit.
  • When all parts of a ship are hit that ship is then destroyed.
  • The winner is determined when all the ships of the opposing player have been destroyed .

2 interface

The interface of the battleships game is as follows:

  • The list of ships shows the ships on the board and which ones are still alive
  • The player flags shows which player is which
  • the grid is to show the position of the coordinates
  • The slider is used to position where on the grid you will be firing
  • The big red fire button is the button you press to fire upo your selected position

3 Missing A Shot

If you miss you should see the following screen

If you miss a shot, especially your first few don't worry as misses are required in this game to help you find the targets anyway.

4 Hitting A Shot

When you finally hit a shot you will be greeted with the following image of an explosion on the screen

Now you have hit a ship you have a great starting point as all ships have at least one other ship part next to them. Thats where you should look to place your next shots.

5 Destroying A Ship

Once you start shooting around the ship eventually you will find the pattern of the ship. after enough shots the ship will be destroyed and a marker will show up on your opponents side with the ship that you destroyed.

6 Winning

Once all enemy ships have been destroyed you are the winner the game is over and you're the best.
